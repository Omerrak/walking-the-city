The Research

One experiment.
Two outcomes.

We ran a randomized controlled field experiment across the Tel Aviv metropolitan area, comparing two custom-built gamified walking apps matched to user personality using the HEXAD framework.

The result: more steps, more civic awareness, and over 2,500 geotagged field reports that tell cities what their residents actually see.
